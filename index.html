Our Story

PASSION FOR AUTHENTIC FOOD

TAQUERIA LOS COMPADRES has been nestled in the SACRAMENTO area ever since 2010. This cozy establishment highlights our passion for market-fresh ingredients, honest cooking, and an enjoyable atmosphere. Our menu features a selection of Authentic Mexican Food dishes, all made in-house by our team of talented chefs. Loved by locals and traveling foodies alike.
